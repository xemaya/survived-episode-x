A pixel art NPC position atlas for a Chinese office life game — 4 NPC archetypes × 4 station positions = 16 full-body sprite cells in a 4x4 grid. Style: SFC/16-bit pixel art, visible pixel grid, no anti-aliasing, limited 16-color palette. Mood: dark humor / 喜丧美学. Consistent with prior reference sheets like A_npc_archetypes_v01.png and B_three_view_v01.png — same characters at same scale.

LAYOUT — 4x4 grid on cream/beige background (#E8E0CC). Each row is one NPC across 4 station positions (left to right: AT_DESK_SIT → AT_DESK_STAND → WALKING → AT_OTHER_LOCATION). Each cell shows the FULL BODY of the NPC at the indicated station. Each cell labeled below in pixel font.
