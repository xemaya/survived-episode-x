A pixel art UI mockup for a Chinese office life game: "the protagonist's monitor screen at the end of the month — HR system email opened, showing the KPI evaluation result." This is the only non-diegetic modal in the game, framed as 笑天 reading his own email on his work computer.

Style: SFC/16-bit pixel art, visible pixel grid, no anti-aliasing on outlines, limited 16-color palette. NOT vector. Mood: dark humor / 喜丧美学 — corporate dread peak.

LAYOUT: Single full-canvas image. The view is the protagonist's computer monitor screen taking up roughly 85% of the 1024x1024 canvas (in 4:3-ish monitor aspect ratio, centered, framed as "fullscreen view of his email"). Around the monitor: faint workstation periphery visible (cubicle wall edges, mug at the very bottom edge, a sticky note corner). The monitor IS the focus.
