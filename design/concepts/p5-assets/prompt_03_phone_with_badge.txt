A pixel art icon for a Chinese office life game: a smartphone face-up on a cubicle desk with a small RED notification badge glowing on the screen edge — the phone is "off" but a push has just landed. Used as a state variant of phone_face_up: the player should look.

Style: SFC/16-bit pixel art, visible pixel grid, no anti-aliasing on outlines, limited 16-color palette. NOT vector. Mood: dark humor / 喜丧美学 — the badge is a tiny red dot of dread on an otherwise dead-quiet screen.

LAYOUT: Single isolated prop sprite, centered in canvas, occupying central 60%. Bottom 1/8 of canvas has minimal wooden desk surface strip. Rest is light off-white background.

CONTENT:
- Phone face-up, top-down 3/4 angle view. Generic black flat smartphone (no brand, no logo).
- Visible features: large black screen (mostly OFF — but now showing a faint banner notification at the top), thin 1px white plastic bezel, home button at bottom, speaker grill at top.
- NEW vs phone_face_up: a small notification banner near the top of the screen — 5x12 px rectangle in #E8E0CC (banner background), with two text-like rows of 1px Chinese pixel-glyphs (illegible at this scale but suggesting "微信" + sender name). At the upper-right corner of the banner is a tiny 3x3 px solid RED dot (#B05050 warmth-red) representing the badge — this is the visual focal point.
- The screen edge has a 1px subtle warmth-red glow halo around the badge area (suggests the "push just arrived" moment, ~2px outward).
- Phone case has the same Mt. Fuji vinyl sticker in the bottom-right corner (4x3 px) for continuity with phone_face_up.
- Phone shadow falls 2-3px to the lower-right onto the desk.
- Background: tiny strip of wooden cubicle desk (#7A5838) at bottom 1/8 with horizontal grain; rest is #E8E0CC.

- 打工人黄 #C8A85A (NOT used — no skin)

VISUAL JOKES (1-3px subtle):
- The notification banner has the tiny silhouette of a WeChat-style square chat icon (4x4 px, generic green-gray, NOT real WeChat colors — just suggestive).
- 1px reflection at top-left corner of screen (overhead fluorescent).
- The red badge dot has a 1px brighter pixel center — suggesting it just lit up.
- A 2x2 px dust speck on the screen.

NO TITLE BANNER (prop sprite).

Output: PNG, 1024x1024, single isolated prop sprite on minimal desk-strip background, 16-bit pixel art, ready for 256x256 or 64x128 downsample. Consistent with phone_face_up sprite (same body) and design/concepts/p5-ui/p5_ui_03_phone_wechat_wang.png mood.
