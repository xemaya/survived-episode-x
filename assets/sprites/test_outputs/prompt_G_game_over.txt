A pixel art game over screen split-panel composition for a Chinese office life game. Two failure-end illustrations side by side, comparing the two ways you can lose. Style: SFC/16-bit pixel art, consistent with prior reference sheets, dark humor / 喜丧美学 (funeral-as-festival aesthetic).

LAYOUT — A vertical center divider splits the canvas into LEFT panel and RIGHT panel. Top of the canvas: a horizontal banner spanning the full width with the title "GAME OVER · 第 X 集终结 / End of Episode X". Bottom of canvas: a small footer with restart hint "[Start] 再来一集 Try Again · [B] 退出 Quit".

LEFT PANEL — "被开除 / Fired" ending:
- The player character — middle-aged Chinese male in navy suit, slightly more rumpled than usual, tie loosened
- Standing OUTSIDE the company building's main entrance, facing away from the building, looking down
- Arms wrapped around a cardboard box of personal items: stainless steel thermos cup (his signature) sticking out, a small green plant, a framed family photo (small pixel detail), the "人才/Talent" mug from HR's desk
- The company building behind him is rendered cold and oppressive (gray-blue #5A7080), the entrance security guard's silhouette visible inside not even watching
- Sky overhead: dark gray-blue overcast, light rain (1-2px diagonal lines)
- A single autumn leaf falling (1px detail)
- His body language: defeated but also subtly relieved — head down but shoulders no longer hunched (the game's hidden joke: getting fired might be the better ending)
- Caption below in pixel font: "你被开除了 / You have been fired" + smaller subtitle: "（其实可能是好事）/ (might actually be good for you)"

- HR in the corner already preparing next month's performance review folder
- Confetti littering the floor (no one will clean it)

CENTER DIVIDER:
- Vertical thin pixel divider with text running down it: "VS" in pixel font, with smaller text "两种结局,同一种地狱 / Two endings, same hell"

PALETTE:
- LEFT panel: cold tones — 灰蓝 #5A7080 dominant, muted #C8A85A skin, gray rain, very limited color
- RIGHT panel: warm tones gone WRONG — 老板金 #E0B050 used aggressively (4-5% pixels, breaking the usual rule on purpose for irony), red-orange accents, harsh #E8E0CC overhead light
- Both panels share the same character (player), same SFC pixel rendering style

VISUAL IDENTITY: art-bible 喜丧美学 to its purest expression. The "celebration" panel is more hellish than the "fired" panel. The visual irony does the storytelling.
