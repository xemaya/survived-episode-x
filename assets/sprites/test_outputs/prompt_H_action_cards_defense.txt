A pixel art trading card sheet for a Chinese office life game — 9 DEFENSE / SLACKING cards in a 3x3 grid. Style: SFC/16-bit pixel art, consistent with prior reference sheets, designed to look like a Yu-Gi-Oh / Hearthstone style card collection but with corporate satire content.

LAYOUT — 3x3 grid of trading cards on a clean cream/white background. Each card has:
- Card frame: pixel art ornate border (cubicle gray-blue #5A7080 base, with old document brown #7A5838 inner trim)
- Card title at top (Chinese primary + English subtitle, pixel font)
- Central illustration (the card's effect, ~70% of card area, showing the player or relevant scene)
- AP cost icon top-left (pixel lightbulb badge with number 1/2/3)
- Card type label bottom: "防御 / DEFENSE" in small text
- Brief flavor text or effect description in pixel font
